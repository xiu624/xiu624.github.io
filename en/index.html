About us

Prims was founded in Munich at end of 2014. It's a professional music studio for young musicians who have a music dream.

We love music, and our definition of music is very extensive. Whether it is classical or folk, oriental or occidental, we always want to interpret it in a new way. Our goal is to spread the culture of new music, so that more people could get to know and appreciate different sorts of music.

At Prims, there are no complex concepts or fancy melodies, we are only a group of happy musicians with our joyful music.
